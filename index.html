What is TL Notes?

Made with NodeJS. TL Notes is an open source web app used to organize tasks in a more efective way based in a traffic light color code.

Say goodbye to procastination.

With the traffic light color system, you will have a visual representation and order of all your tasks. With constant notifications we can assure an easier control of your time.
